1. Introduction

1.1 Welcome to XMAME

This the the *nix port of the almost legendary mame. Mame is an arcade machine emulator, started in 1997 by Nicola Salmoria. It started out as a series of emulators for individual games. This series of emulators was combined into a simgle multi-game emulator. This is the current form of mame; no longer a one-man show, there are over 100 contributors to the project.

1.2 Keep everything up-to-date!

The most recent copy of this guide, the xmame-sources and many more can be found on the xmame project homepage at http://x.mame.net/
